DentaDesk is a cloud-based software platform that allows dentist, dental labs and manufacturing sites to streamline their digital workflow.
With a complete and easy-to-use system we have created a seamless workflow along the digital value chain - from the model to product.

For dentists

The transfer of your data to a reliable lab and manufacturing partner is essential in order to achieve excellent treatment results. DentaDesk supports you in the transfer as well as the exchange of treatment data as for instance 3D models of intra-oral scanners.

For labs

Especially for dental technicians the uncomplicated transfer of production data to milling and production centers often is a time-consuming and error-prone process. With DentaDesk your files can be transferred easily via drag and drop in the web browser. Apart from STLs also construction data can be imported directly from CAD solutions.

For production centers

The (mass-)production of completely individual products confronts many production centers with entirely new challenges. Particularly the automation of workflows - from the data reception to the delivery note and billing - has to function as efficiently as possible in order to guarantee an economically valuable result. DentaDesk includes a completely integrated ERP solution for warehouse management, order management and billing, which can even be adapted to indivdual needs.

For system integrators

DentaDesk is based on a modular technology approach as well as on an open business model. We offer technology partners as well as resellers the opportunity to offer their own products based on our system or even sell DentaDesk as a "white label" solution with their own name and design. Features

DentaDesk is an open system with flexible posibilities for customization.
Here are some of the most important features:

File Sharing

One of the core features of DentaDesk lies in the uncomplicated transfer of project data. This way, relevant data can be exchanged between all project participants both fast and efficiently.

STL Processing

Uploaded STL data is shown diretly in the browser in a 3D preview. STLs can equally be checked on the server side and post-processed.

Customer Communication

Especially given a growing customer base the communication via phone or e-mail often shows itself as impractical - after all, everyone in the organisation needs to be up-to-date on what is going on. For this reason DentaDesk comprises a completely integrated messaging system, which allow project participants to internally exchange messages about a project.

Billing

After successful production DentaDesk can quickly produce respective delivery and billing documments as PDF. Through their DentaDesk account, customers have an overview of all their billing documents as well as their open balance.

ERP System

Incoming orders are downloaded automatically in the production center and can be forwarded to external systems, such as for instance CAM. The complete further processing of the order the follows in the ERP system of DentaDesk. Here, among other things, you can capture serial or charge numbers, create delivery notes or check order data.

Process Automation

The ERP system behind DentaDesk is completely adaptable and can be extended flexibly with new interface features. With this external systems can be linked dynamically in order to automate processing as much as possible.
