DentaDesk is a cloud-based software platform that allows dentists, dental labs and manufacturing sites to streamline their digital workflow.
With a complete and easy-to-use system we have created a seamless workflow along the digital value chain - from the model to product.

For dentists

The transfer of your data to a reliable lab and manufacturing partner is essential in order to achieve excellent treatment results. DentaDesk supports you in the transfer as well as the exchange of treatment data as for instance 3D models of intra-oral scanners.

For labs

With DentaDesk your files can be transferred easily to milling and production centers via drag and drop in the web browser. This uncomplicated data transfer will no longer be time-consuming and error-prone process for dental technicians. Apart from STLs also construction data can be imported directly from CAD solutions

For production centers

DentaDesk includes a completely integrated ERP solution for warehouse management, order management and billing, which can even be adapted to individual needs. The (mass-)production of completely individual products confronts many production centers with entirely new challenges. Dentadesk ensures efficient automation of workflows - from the data reception to the delivery note and billing - to guarantee an economically valuable result.

For system integrators

DentaDesk is based on a modular technology approach as well as on an open business model. We offer technology partners as well as resellers the opportunity to offer their own products based on our system or even sell DentaDesk as a "white label" solution with their own name and design. Features

DentaDesk is an open system with flexible posibilities for customization.
Here are some of the most important features:

File Sharing

Fast and efficient exchange of project data between all participants is one of the core features of Dentadesk.

STL Processing

Uploaded STL data is shown directly in the browser in a 3D preview. STLs can equally be checked on the server side and post-processed.

Customer Communication

A completely integrated messaging system, which allows project participants to internally exchange messages about a project. Now everyone in the organization stays up-to-date on what is going on.

Billing

After successful production DentaDesk can quickly produce respective delivery and billing documments as PDF. Through their DentaDesk account, customers have an overview of all their billing documents as well as their open balance.

ERP System

An automated order processing starts from downloading of incoming orders in the production center with forwarding options to external systems, such as CAM, and is being completed in the ERP system of DentaDesk. Here you can also capture serial or charge numbers, create delivery notes or check order data.

Process Automation

The ERP system behind DentaDesk is completely adaptable and can be extended flexibly with new interface features. With this external systems can be linked dynamically in order to automate processing as much as possible.
